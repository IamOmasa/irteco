    WHO WE ARE

    IRTECO has worked on several organizations, Government, NGOs and Private Sector. It has greatly succeeded in building a strong team of expertise with the perfect blend of Tanzanians and international experts in the crop production sector. IRTECO undertakes irrigation management and economic empowerment roles and provides technical and financial advice to a wide range of smallholder farmers in the common champion model of production, value addition, marketing and policy linkages that small scale farmers development package including agriculture and institutional aspects are well incorporated.

    Mision

    Leading organization in mobilizing and capacity building services to target population (mainly women and youths) on integrated water resource management and economic empowerment (with gender mainstreaming) in Tanzania.

    Vision

    A strong organization gathers for people-centered and equitable access, utilization and management of water for their food security and guarantees their representation in related to local and international forums.
